🍪 Chaturbate Cookie

Required for Discovery feature. Get your cookie from chaturbate.com (F12 → Application → Cookies → csrftoken)

📋 How to get your cookie?
  1. Open chaturbate.com in your browser
  2. Press F12
  3. Go to Application tab → Cookies
  4. Find and copy the csrftoken value
  5. Paste it here as: csrftoken=YOUR_VALUE
